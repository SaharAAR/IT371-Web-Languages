   H&S cafe    Our aim is to share the passion of Specialty coffee with the Arab world, with carefully selected, cupped, rated and properly stored
green beans. We import our beans from known lots and auctions, where the beans are ethically and fairly traded. We roast our beans
lightly to show the spectrum of characteristics each bean offers. Each coffee is carefully analysed to find the perfect roast profile, on our fluid bed roasters.

  Our Blends
Summer Solstice

"Sometimes magic happens when two single origins come together. We're getting plenty of floral aromatics and light berry sweetness in this beautifully clean cup."
Persephone

"Intelligentsia's blend has a round mouthfeel with a smooth, candied sweetness. Flavors of citrus and stone fruits meet hints of creamy nougat and caramel, making a thoroughly enjoyable first cup of the day."
Otoño

"Our Otoño blend marks the change of seasons with fresh-crop coffees from Guatemala, Costa Rica, and Honduras that together deliver the flavour of fall: apple, brown sugar and plum."
Borealis

"Whether you glimpse the Northern Lights this winter or not, the persistent sweetness and fruit flavors in Borealis will warm your body and fire your mind."
